RELEVANCE

Relevance assesses how well the Practicum Program aligns with students' career goals and industry needs, ensuring practical applicability and bridging the gap between theory and practice.

SCOPE OF WORK

The scope of work encompasses the range and depth of tasks and responsibilities undertaken during the Practicum Program, providing students with hands-on experience and opportunities to apply theoretical knowledge.

CAREER DEVELOPMENT

Career development within the Practicum Program involves acquiring skills, knowledge, and experiences relevant to students' chosen career paths, fostering professional growth, and facilitating connections with potential employers for future success.
