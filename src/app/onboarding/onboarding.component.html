Onboarding

Practicum Coordinator Guidelines

This guide will walk you through the essential steps and processes you'll need to follow throughout the term. The guide is divided into four main sections: Prior to the Start of the Term, Pre-Deployment, Deployment, and Post-Deployment.

Prior to the Start of the Term

1. Create a New Batch

Ensure new students signing up for the incoming term are assigned to the correct batch.

• Navigate to "Batch".

• Click on "Add Batch".

• Enter the batch details (i.e. Minimum hours required).

• Add the new batch.

 2. Create and Publish Requirements

Set up the necessary requirements for students.

• Go to the "Requirements" tab under the "Document Hub" module.

• If the requirement does not exist yet, click on "Add Requirement".

• Provide the name of the requirement (i.e. Title).

• Once saved, edit the details further (e.g. File template, Due Date, Deployment Phase) by selecting the pencil icon on the right.

• Publish the requirement by ticking on the checkbox on the left.

3. Create Announcements

Disseminate important information to students.

• Open the "Announcements" module.

• Click on "Create Announcement".

• Enter the announcement details (Subject, Content, and Relevant batch).

• Post the announcement.

• You may verify which batch the announcement was posted to based on the tag under the Subject heading.

4. Update Companies in the Company List

Keep the company list updated for job searching.

• Access the "Company List" module.

• Click on "Add Company" on the top right or select an existing company to modify.

• Enter or update the company details.

• Save the changes.

• You may also delete companies but will only be successful if there are no active students or group chats associated with the company.

5. Provide Guidance Using the Job Matching Module

Assist students in finding suitable internship opportunities.

• Navigate to the "Job Matching" module.

• Review student profiles and the top company recommendations made per student.

• View the complete list of recommended companies by navigating to each student profile.

Pre-Deployment

1. Check Unchecked Submissions

Review and process student submissions.

• Check for any notification alerts for the "Document Hub".

• Send feedback for any revisions. Do not update the status yet as you will be forwarding the file to OULC for approval.

• Resubmissions will automatically be under the same requirement of the student and increment their file version submission.

2. Update Submissions through OULC Tab

Manage pre-deployment document submissions.

• Once the OULC has made their comments for revision or decision to approve, you may update the document status of the student.

• Access the "Document Hub" and click on the "OULC" tab.

• Navigate to the file submission of the student and update the document status (e.g. Approved, Disapproved) along with the relevant feedback.

• Send the feedback made.

Deployment

1. Communicate with Students

Maintain communication with students during their internships.

• Check the side navigation bar for any notification alerts in the inbox, signifying new messages.

• Open the "Inbox" module.

• Create direct messages or group chats for students deployed under the same company.

• Use these channels to provide support and gather feedback.

Post-Deployment

1. Check Company Evaluations

Review student feedback on their internship experiences.

• Access the "Company Evaluations" module.

• View the reports page for data visualization of student responses.

• Analyze the data to understand student experiences and identify any issues.

2. Use the MOA DSS

Decide on retaining partnerships with companies.

• Open the "MOA DSS" module.

• Review the decision support system's suggestions based on set criteria for all companies.

• Navigate to individual company pages to have a more detailed breakdown of the factors that led to the decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

This page will cover the questions regarding the various functionalities of the system. If there are any further clarfications, consult with your practicum coordinator.

What should I do after my first login into PracTrack?

You should first navigate to your settings in order to set additional details to your account such as your location, preferred work setup, allowance type, industry, and job position which will be used for another module called “Job Matching”.

Where can I see the things I need to accomplish and posted announcements?

The dashboard will provide you with things you need to give attention to the most. This will show you your current deployment phase alongside other details. To view announcements, you can navigate to the “Announcements” module on the side navigation bar.

How do I find an internship company using PracTrack?

You can find prospective companies through the “Company Listing” module. The table provides a comprehensive list of companies DLSU has active partnerships with and relevant information such as the point of contact when interested to apply, whether the company provides allowance, and the job positions offered among other details as well.

How do I submit requirements?

Navigating to the “Document Hub” module will show a list of all the published requirements by the coordinator. By default, it will display the phase you are currently deployed to. You may submit by clicking on the desired requirement and can either upload a file or download the template file first to accomplish it. Additionally, you may download template files from succeeding phases if you want to prepare ahead of time.

How will I know if my submissions have been approved already?

There will be notifications on the side navigation bar once there have been changes to the status of your submissions. If you navigate to the “Document Hub” module, you may also see the individual document statuses for each of your submissions there.

How do I contact my coordinator?

You may communicate with your practicum coordinator through the “Inbox” module. Do take note that you may only create chat messages with your practicum coordinator and not with other students.

What if my chosen internship company is not listed?

You may communicate with your practicum coordinator through the “Inbox” module regarding what steps are needed to accomplish.

What should I do if I want to leave my internship company in the middle of my deployment?

In the “Document Hub” module, you may select on the “Request to Migrate” which will make an appeal to your coordinator if you will be transferring to a different company. Take note that the coordinator can either reject, accept and retain your current rendered hours, or accept and reset your rendered hours. Please discuss the circumstances of your decision with your coordinator once you have sent a request.
